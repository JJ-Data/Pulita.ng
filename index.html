Switching to LPG and CNG Power Sources

The High cost of Disel leads to high electricity costs,
By setting up independent CNG/LPG power plants, we can save 58-72% of the cost and quickly recover the upfront investment in natural gas infrastructure.

LPG Generator Advantage

LPG is widely distributed in Nigeria, it is mainly composed of butane and propane. From small household cylinders to large industrial tanks, LPG is easy to store and use, it's portability and convinence make it an attractive alternative to diesel power generation.

CNG Generator Advantage

Natural gas is more enviromentally friendly and can effectively reduce carbon emissions, Nigeria has abundant natural gas reserves. The Nigeria government is currently carrying out large-scale pipeline constrution to strenghten the distribution of natural gas in the country
