Mobile Daughter Gas Refill Station

The daughter gas refill station consists of gas cylinder group, compressor, dispenser, gas unloading column, metering system, gas filter, gas leakage alarming system, and 20ft or 40ft container.

This series of daughter gas filling stations can be fixed or mobile, with a capacity of 600-3000 standard cubic meters of natural gas that also designed with a 250 bar compressors and gas dispensers, which can effectively meet the gas filling needs of small vehicles. Compared with traditional gas stations, it is lower cost and more flexible.
